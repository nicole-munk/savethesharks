THANKS FOR PLAYING

Now that you understand the three main things that make sharks unhappy, you can start doing things to make them happy!

Here is a list of things you can do to support the cause!

  • Avoid plastic and recycle.
  • Keep sharks off the menu, avoid eating fish if you can, or buy "sustainable seafood" (more info below).
  • Spread the word! Share on social media using the #protectouroceans and keep educating yourself about this.

For more information, you can visit the following webpages:

  • Sustainable Seafood Guide
  • Oceana
  • Bycatch
  • Project Aware
  • WWF
