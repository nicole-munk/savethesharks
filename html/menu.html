There are three main human activities that endanger shark species:

  • Overfishing: Catching too many fish at once, so that the breeding population becomes too depleted to recover (no food for sharks!).
  • Bycatch: Incidental capture and mortality of non-target marine animals during fishing.
  • Destruction of the ocean: Pollution and climate change are destroying our oceans, endangering species that depend on the stability of these.
